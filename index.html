Terradore
Terradore Paxia-Baker / The Modern Prophet
Terradore reigns as the archbishop of Amar. A titan of leadership and a masterful artisan of confectionery, his age spans eons, leaving his past shrouded in mystery. Being the adopted son of the renowned Stoney Stoner and blessed by The Great Baker himself, Terradore's influence extends far beyond the borders of Amar. He laid the foundation for the World's Cultural Guild, an organization orchestrating global celebrations and fairs of unparalleled grandeur. As Eldham's twilight loomed, Terradore was shown a vision; The promised land of Abexilas, where his legacy of leadership and culinary mastery continues to thrive, casting a delectable spell over all who dwell in Amar.
Shoddy
Shoddy Aurelius Paxia-Baker / The Golden Boy
As the nation of Amar flourished under the stewardship of Terradore, the need for additional assistance became apparent. Drawing upon his divine lineage and culinary prowess, Terradore embarked on a momentous endeavor. Infused with his own blood, the essence of the great baker, and the regal blood of the Rat King, he baked a cupcake of unparalleled magnificence. From this delectable creation, a new sprinkle emerged into the world: the enigmatic Shoddy Aurelius Paxia-Baker. With his innate ability to shapeshift and unwavering loyalty to Amar, he has ensured the nation's prosperity and stability over the years.
Zebulonus
Zebulonus Baker / The Last Snowman
Zebulonus, the last living snowman of his kind, once resided in the ancient world of Eldham. His unparalleled genius brought forth the designs of the majestic Pastel Cathedral and the grand headquarters of the WCG. However, as Eldham's end grew near, Zebulonus, along with the remaining Margraves, left for the new promised land: The world of Abexilas. With vast amounts of new land the seeds of his architectural brilliance, sown in the past, have begun to flourish, heralding a new era of wonder and splendor for all of Amar.
Zolgo
Zolgo Baker / An "Imaginary" Friend
As Zebulonus dwelled in his basement abode, yearning for companionship amidst the icy stone walls, Zolgo emerged from his imagination as his steadfast companion. When The Great Baker transported the Margraves to Abexilas, he bestowed upon Zolgo the ultimate gift; a transformation from mere figment to tangible existence. Baked into a living being, Zolgo found purpose alongside his creator, Zebulonus, delving into the realm of architectural design. Together, they weave dreams into reality, crafting the grandeur of Magdalena's skyline. Zolgo plays a pivotal role in shaping the architectural marvels that grace the world of Abexilas, ensuring that Amar's legacy endures through the ages.
